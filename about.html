My story

When I was 2, I found out the Christmas man was nothing but an illusion as I recognized my uncle in the costume.

When I was 3, my parents divorced.

When I was 4, my uncle, who I’ve been close to, died in a motorbike accident.

When I was 5, we moved to Germany from Belarus.

When I was 6, I was bullied in school.

When I was 7 and 8, I lived a happy life.

When I was 9, my mother and I have been emotionally abused by my stepfather. We also lived in the lived in the homeless shelter for a few weeks.

When I was 10, I spent a few days in the hospital with shingles, I was released at Christmas.

When I was 11, I got in trouble for bulling two classmates, that’s the lowest I’ve ever been.

When I was 12, I lived a pointless life and hated it.

When I was 13, I started my first business and I got on self-improvement.

When I was 14, I went from 8th grade straight to 10th grade, while working on my business full time.

When I was 15, I took a short break from my business, so I created 375 short timelapse and slow-motion videos in 60 days. A few months later, I had my first product online, a mental math app. I started to feel alive for the first time. I replaced every bad habit with a good one, for example, I started to do 150 pushups and 50 pullups a day.

When I was 16, I moved on from past pain and became social. I published two more mental math apps. I lived for months without warm water, sleeping in, music, or sweets. I started my self-improvement channel and my blog. I solved 10.000 mental math tasks in 11 hours and recorded it, because I wanted to promote my apps and to have a cool story. I asked out the same girl four times and saw a year of heartbreak. I saw depression and burnout after my first business failed after 5.000 invested hours. I started to focus on videos next, I created 50 videos in 90 days. I also finished 12th grade with a perfect high school diploma. I wrote 400.000 words in 500 days because I started to get up at 3. I ran 10 km in 39 minutes and recorded 10 videos in 13 hours the next day.

When I was 17, I got rejected 50 times in 4 days in person. I recorded 126 in total and lost any desire to continue creating videos, which my second failed business. At 17, I also rested a lot. I also made peace with my mother.

Now I'm right before university.

I am so calm because of what lies behind.
