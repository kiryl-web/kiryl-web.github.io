If my life were to be summarized in a single sentence, what would it be?

Uncompromising excellence
is a true testament to the indomitability
of my spirit.

Yet, for some time, I've felt a distance between my ideals and my actions. I failed to embody my own ideals. I stopped demonstrating uncompromising excellence. Hence, I decided to put my blog offline for an uncertain amount of time.

If you're still interested in exploring my previous thoughts, you can find them here.

Thank you for your understanding, and I hope to share more with you in the future.

I need to take a deep breath.

September 1st, 2024
